Donations

Because of the large volume of text that is synthesized on a daily basis, the costs of running WSBSynth is quite high.

So if you find WSBSynth useful and want to help cover costs, feel free to make a donation.

The progress bar on the homepage represents the percentage of our donation goal that has been reached for the given month.
